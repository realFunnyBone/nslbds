An unofficial sequel to New Super Mario Bros. Wii, created by a team of devoted Nintendo fans. Playable legally on any homebrew-enabled Wii; no piracy or hardware mods needed.


Game Features

We won't ruin all of the surprises, but here's a peek...

  • 130 New Levels

    Mamma-mia! Clear your schedule and prepare to join Mario on the long adventure that lies ahead

  • All New Worlds

    Explore new worlds with Mario, from underground sewers to tall mountaintops, and beyond!

  • Customised Goodies

    Original bosses, new cut-scenes, backgrounds, tilesets and a plumber's weight in new content has been added!
